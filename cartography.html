Map Name: Ascent

Ascent is a visually stunning map set in an Italian cityscape with a combination of both modern and classical architecture. It features wide-open spaces, narrow corridors, and various levels of elevation, providing a diverse and dynamic battlefield for players.

ASCENT: FIRST LIGHT

10 years after First Light in the Italian city of Venice, an agent of Omega made the journey across worlds to plant and detonate a spike. With the VALORANT Protocol failing to stop them, the resulting explosion triggered a cataclysmic event, ripping a huge chunk of the city out of the ground. Now permanently suspended hundreds of meters in the sky, this airborne island remains as a product of the most destructive radianite-related event known to Alpha.

Map Name: Breeze

Breeze is a visually striking map set in a tropical island paradise. It features wide-open spaces, long sightlines, and a mixture of indoor and outdoor areas. The map's size sets it apart from many other Valorant maps, making it unique in terms of gameplay.

BREEZE: MYSTERIOUS ISLAND

Within the area of the Atlantic Ocean known as the Bermuda Triangle lies a mysterious island. With a radianite mine constructed beneath, a cargo ship beached and in pieces on its shores, and a teleporter operating with unintended functionality, there's much to uncover at this tropical paradise that's of strategic importance to both its homeworld and invaders from Omega.

Map Name: Haven

Haven is a unique and asymmetrical map located in an undisclosed European location. Unlike other Valorant maps, Haven features three bombsites instead of the usual two, making it distinct and challenging for both attackers and defenders.

HAVEN: THIMPU

In Bhutan's capital city of Thimphu, a temple is subjected to the latest developments facing a modern Earth in the era of radianite. As parts of it crumble and burn due to a radianite-related event causing masses of large columns to erupt from the ground, the global reach of Kingdom Corporation has also arrived there, taking over the site to use it as storage for their radianite crates.

Map Name: Sunset

Sunset is one of VALORANT's more traditional maps with two sites and three lanes. Its one additional feature is a mechanical door found between B Market and Mid Courtyard. Players can use a switch on the Market side of the door to close or open it. This door is impenetrable while closed but has 500 HP, allowing players to damage and eventually destroy it. Once destroyed, the doorway remains permanently open for the rest of the round.

SUNSET: BOYLE HEIGHTS

In the Los Angeles neighborhood of Boyle Heights, residents were already noticing strange things occurring around a secretive Kingdom facility located in the area, with peculiar creatures spotted running around and unusually large sounds coming from nearby. Now, a huge sinkhole that has suddenly opened up in front of the facility has put the whole neighborhood at risk, as Kingdom desperately attempts to prevent any details about the sinkhole's cause from being revealed and stop the facility's classified work from being exposed.
